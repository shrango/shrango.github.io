I am a second-year PhD student at Washington University in St. Louis (WUSTL), luckily advised by Prof. Jiaxin Huang.

Before that, I received my Master degree at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT/CAS) in Jul. 2024, where I studied at ICT Natural Language Processing (ICTNLP) group. I received my B.E. degree in Data Science and Big Data Technology from University of International Business and Economics (UIBE) in Jun. 2021.

Research

My research interests mainly lie in Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models. Particularly, I am interested in:

  • Large Language Model Reasoning.
  • Efficient language model generation, Speculative Decoding, Efficient in-context learning.
  • Multilingual large language model, Machine Translation.
